Red Bull x Moxy Bike

This custom-built tricycle gave Moxy Hotel guests and meeting attendees a full Red Bull experience, with integrated speakers, LED lights, and of course all the bar prep for a great drink to go.
Red Bull asked for a bar on wheels and after showing them a few concepts, I worked with a small team including our Creative Director, the fabricators, and account service teams from all sides to bring this idea to life.
The fabricators and I created a blueprint for the build and sourced accesories to go with the overall theme. Creative solutions were implemented like the top window display showing off Red Bull cans and a flip top to create more counter space for those Red Bull and vodkas.
