About

My Story

I’m a designer based in Cleveland, Ohio working at Brokaw, Inc. I’ve been a professional designer almost ten years now, but have been living that designer life since day one. (Just ask 4-year-old me about her homemade t-shirt dress.)
I have worked on everything from print ads to full scale campaigns to animated social posts. Some of my clients include GE Lighting, Philips, Purell, Smokey Bones, and Nestle. I love seeing projects come to life, big and small.
In my free time I enjoy hiking with my dog, embroidery, and being a mom to two little girls.
