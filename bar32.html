Bar 32 Logo

When Hilton Cleveland Downtown decided to create a stunning cut out on the 32nd floor of their new build, they asked us to create a name and brand identity for the space. The name and numbers were custom created to mirror the physical space where the bar is located, an open-air patio standing between two floors near the top of the building.
I worked with our Creative Director and copywriter to create this logo, along with a full team tasked with multiple restaurant identities for the new hotel.
